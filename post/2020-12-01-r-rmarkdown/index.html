Box-plot showing the range of Final Exam scores of Statistics students

statscores = c(57, 66, 69, 71, 72, 73, 74, 77, 78, 78, 79, 79, 81, 81, 82, 83, 83, 88, 89, 94)

abline(v=m, col="blue")
